== DESCRIPTION ==

Effortlessly design and build your perfect theme with our intuitive tools—no coding skills are needed. Transform your vision into reality quickly and easily, with a seamless process that lets you focus on what matters most: creating a stunning and unique theme tailored to your needs.

<a href="https://gutenverse.com/theme-list/" target="_blank" rel="">Explore examples of pre-built template websites we built using Gutenverse Themes Builder</a>

== FEATURES ==

= Multiple Themes Creation =
You can effortlessly create and manage multiple themes within a single site, eliminating the need to switch between different sites. This powerful feature allows you to experiment with various designs, test different looks, and seamlessly integrate diverse themes—all from one convenient location

= Multiple Templates Version =
You can easily incorporate up to two distinct versions of templates within a single theme. For instance, you might use core templates for a versatile, general layout while also integrating Gutenverse templates that require the Gutenverse plugin.

= Manage Patterns =
Build a diverse collection of patterns for your themes, allowing you to customize and enhance your design with a variety of unique and versatile options.

= Manage Assets =
Streamline your workflow and maintain complete control over your styles and scripts, ensuring optimal performance and seamless integration with your themes.

= One Click Bundle =
Build and bundle your theme with a single click, streamlining the process into a simple and efficient action. Enjoy the ease of transforming your design into a fully packaged theme ready for deployment, all with minimal effort.

== Installation ==

Installing Gutenverse Themes Builder is a breeze. Follow one of the two methods below:

= From the WordPress Plugin Dashboard: =
1. Go to the WordPress plugin dashboard and search for "Gutenverse Themes Builder."
2. Click on the "Install Now" button next to the Gutenverse Themes Builder plugin.
3. Once installed, activate the plugin from your WordPress Dashboard.

= Using the Upload Method: =
1. Download the Gutenverse Themes Builder plugin as a zip file from the official website.
2. In your WordPress Dashboard, navigate to "Plugins" and click on "Add New."
3. Select the "Upload Plugin" button and choose the Gutenverse Themes Builder zip file.
4. Click on "Install Now" and activate the plugin after installation.

For additional information and assistance, please visit our extensive <a href="https://gutenverse.com/docs/" target="_blank" rel="">Documentation</a> section.
